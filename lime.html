Lime Margaritas

Prep Time: 2 minutes

Total Time: 2 minutes

Ingredients

1 1/2 oz. reposado tequila
3/4 oz. Monin Lime Syrup
2 oz. sweet & sour mix
ice

Instructions


1. Combine ingredients in shaker in the order listed
2. Cap and shake vigorously
3. Strain into chilled serving glass
4. Garnish
